DevOps

DevOps name is a combination of two words: Development and Operations. Plan, code, build, test, release, deploy, operate and monitor. This is a never-ending cycle and the logo of DevOps makes perfect sense to this. These tools has been categorized into various stages of DevOps.

Amazon Web Services (AWS) is a cloud business of the famous retail company amazon.com, allows aws clients to automate their infrastructure and manage resources with less efforts. It provides reliable services at low cost which is one of the key reasons for its popularity.
